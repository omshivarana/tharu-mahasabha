Supporting the Rana Tharu community entails a multi-faceted approach encompassing education, health, freedom, and beyond. Our vision is to establish comprehensive educational programs tailored to their cultural context, empowering Rana Tharu youth with knowledge and skills. We strive to ensure accessible healthcare services, addressing prevalent health issues and promoting well-being. Championing their rights and freedoms, we advocate for legal and social reforms to eliminate discrimination and ensure equal opportunities. Sustainable development initiatives foster economic growth while preserving their traditional way of life. Through collaborative efforts, we envision a future where Rana Tharu individuals thrive in a society that values their heritage, health, and rights.

All About Tharu Mahasabha By Kishan Rana

Supporting the Rana Tharu community encompasses a holistic approach across various domains. In education, our vision is to establish schools that not only teach academic subjects but also incorporate Rana Tharu language and culture, fostering a sense of pride and identity. We aim to improve healthcare accessibility by building clinics and training local healthcare workers, addressing prevalent diseases and promoting preventive care. Upholding their freedom involves advocating for land rights, legal representation, and protection from exploitation. Economic empowerment initiatives, such as vocational training and microfinance programs, enable self-sufficiency and reduce poverty. Environmental conservation efforts preserve their ancestral lands and natural resources. Ultimately, our vision is of a thriving Rana Tharu community, where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, freedom, and opportunities for sustainable development, while their rich cultural heritage is celebrated and preserved for future generations.
