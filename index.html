About

This is the community of, Tharu Mahasabha

The primary goal of Tharu Mahasabha is to enhance the prosperity and development of the Tharu community. Our aim is to improve education, health, and economic conditions among Tharu people. We aspire to preserve and promote Tharu culture, language, and heritage. Our objective is to foster social equality and equal opportunities, where every individual receives respect and support for their rights. We work to improve the social, economic, and political status of the Tharu community. We strive for equality of rights and opportunities in society, and are committed to assisting the Tharu community in leading prosperous and progressive lives, establishing a strong position in society through Tharu Mahasabha.
